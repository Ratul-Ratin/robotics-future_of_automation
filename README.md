# robotics-future_of_automation
Field research report on robotics by Team Pi-THOR, IUBAT- CSE Department

> Course: ART 1101 – Educational Planning | Spring 2026

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project documents our team's field visit to a leading robotics innovation hub in Mirpur, Dhaka, Bangladesh. The goal was to explore how modern robotics technologies — including AI, IoT, ROS, and embedded systems — are being applied in real-world educational and research environments.

We observed humanoid robots, autonomous drones, self-navigating vehicles, and gesture-controlled systems, and analyzed the technologies powering them.

---

## 👥 Team Pi-THOR

## 🔍 Key Findings

- **Hands-On Learning** — Project-based robotics training is far more effective than theory alone
- **AI Integration** — Machine learning enables robots to recognize objects and make decisions with minimal human input
- **IoT Connectivity** — Real-time device communication makes automation systems smarter and faster
- **Local Innovation** — In-house R&D and local hardware manufacturing are driving Bangladesh's tech growth
- **Tech Convergence** — AI + IoT + Industry 4.0 is the roadmap for next-gen engineers
